DEV.INC.CO
Coordinators: Shivansh Malasi, Shubham Pandey, Ashish Pandey
April 22, 2019
Behind every successful business, there is a good UI and a good UX. At dev.inc.co we value innovation in the design and its implementation. Unlike some other development competition, we at Kaivalya don’t have any limits or any restrictions. Using any of the technologies like Android, ASP.NET, etc. we want participants to develop an innovative solution based on a given theme which will be judged on the basis of all factors like UI, UX and efficiency.

DEV.INC.CO is a team event which is conducted in BTKIT Campus with a single round. The main motive is to find innovation among creative minds by judging their work based on the themes provided to them.

Enroll yourself and show the world that you’re the Da Vinci of Code

COMPETITION RULES:
  • “Dev.inc.co” will be conducted onsite in the Campus of BTKIT Dwarahat.
  • It is a team event and a team can consist of up to 3 members.
  • We only allow college students to participate and hence a certificate and prizes will be given to winners.
  • The event will go live on the commencement day of Kaivalya.
  • Winners will be decided on the basis of overall performance of the solution
  • The decision of the jury remains final while deciding the winners for the same.
