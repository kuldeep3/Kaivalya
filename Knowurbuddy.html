Know Your Buddy
Coordinators: Bharat Bhushan Pandey, Devbrath Singh
April 22, 2019
‘Know your buddy’ is a pursuit of trivial knowledge about your computer system, to encourage students to achieve excellence in learning the system properties which increases their connection with their system.

The quiz is typically played with a lockout buzzer system between some numbers of teams, most commonly teams of two players each. The moderator reads questions to the teams, whose players endeavor to buzz first with the correct answer, scoring points for their team.

With science and technology growing at such an alarming rate, facts and figures just keep adding up in the computer hardware. To match the influx of this humongous amount of data we need a brain that is sharp as a razor and pliable as a rubber band. And the quest for the search of this perfect brain ends at technical quiz, and being a geek a quiz will best if it is based on computer hardware! With a wide array of questions from numerous problems, this is one event which will breach the boundaries of your system love.

COMPETITION RULES:
  • “Know your buddy” will be conducted onsite in BTKIT Dwarahat Campus.
  • It is a team event and a team can consist of up to 2 members.
  • We only allow college students to participate and hence a certificate and prizes will be given to winners.
  • There will be only one round in which some tasks are to be performed on their respective laptops to show how well they know their system.
  • Each task has marks associated to it and team with maximum marks will be deemed winner.
  • The decision of the jury remains final while deciding the winners for the same.
