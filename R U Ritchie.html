R U Ritchie
Coordinators Vikram Kumar, Hrishabh Gaur
April 15, 2019
Show us your coding skill in the genesis of Kaivalya — “GAMBIT”. Organized in the higher computing environment of BTKIT, gambit is a coding competition having the following rules:
  • The contest is open to anyone with a knack for programming.
  • It is a team contest with each team having a maximum of two members from the same institution.
  • Teams will be ranked as per the number of problems solved.
  • Ties will be broken by the total time for each user in ascending order of time.
  • The event constitutes of qualiﬁers’ and the programmers’ stage.
  • Qualiﬁers will be open to everyone who has a knack of coding and selected contestants will compete in the programmers’ stage.
