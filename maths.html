Mathematica
Coordinators: Pulkit Joshi, Sandeep Singh Bhaisora
April 23, 2019
Speed!! Speed!! Speed!!
Mathematica is a game of questions and answers on all problem solving aspects that is open for all. The event will typically organize onsite. There will be several problems that need to be solved by participants.

With science and technology growing at such an alarming rate, facts and figures just keep adding up. To match the influx of this humongous amount of data we need a brain that is sharp as a razor and pliable as a rubber band. And the quest for the search of this perfect brain ends at solving math problems! With a wide array of questions from numerous technical topics, this is one event which will breach the boundaries of your technical love.

COMPETITION RULES:
  • “MATHEMATICA” will be conducted onsite in BTKIT Dwarahat Campus.
  • It is an individual event and teams aren’t allowed.
  • We only allow college students to participate and hence a certificate and prizes will be given to winners.
  • There will be only one round with some multiple choice questions.
  • Participant with maximum marks will be deemed winner.
  • The decision of the jury remains final while deciding the winners for the same.
