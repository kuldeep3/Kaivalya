Monkhub
Coordinators: Gaurav Pant, Aditya Singh
April 23, 2019
For all of us, Computer Geeks, “C” is the mother tongue. C, being the legendary programming language can be well described as the hardest to learn as well. This event challenges you to prove your mastery over C.

In “MONKHUB”, you get multiple choice questions on C and you have to answer them correctly and accurately. This is an onsite event organized in BTKIT Campus. This is an individual event and the participant with maximum score will be the winner. Each wrong answer can also cost you a penalty, so better be accurate. C, although taught in most basic of the programming courses, yet believed to be hardest of the languages to learn, is itself an art. And if you have that art, then exhibit us in “MONKHUB”, which by no means is an easy challenge.

COMPETITION RULES:
  • It is an individual event and teams aren’t allowed.
  • We only allow college students to participate and hence a certificate and prizes will be given to winners
  • There will be only one round with some multiple choice questions
  • Participant with maximum marks will be deemed winner.
